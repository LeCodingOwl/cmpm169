Experiment 5 - 3D Graphics

Imitate

I was really fascinated by the terrain project made by Taigo and it gave me the idea of using audio to control the looks of the grid. I created a simple project that would allow me to experiment with sound. In the simple audio project, I created boxes that would stretch based on the sound.

Integrate

After tweaking and fiddling around with the codes on both projects, I merge the two and continue making adjustments to the code. The waves reminded me of water and ocean so I thought of trying to make a retro style background, but I could not get the image of the retro sun to work with my idea, scraping the idea and kept the project simple with a blue grid instead. I made more adjustments to the code so that it would be easier to visualize the effects of the audio.

Innovate

I first started off with tinkering the Terrain project by Taigo and learned how their project worked. Afterwards I created a simple project that allowed me to experiment with sound and how I could use it to manipulate the grid. Then I combined the two projects together to create a grid that was affected by audio. After tweaking and changing some of the variables, I managed to create what you see in the last image in the Imitate section.
