About Me

Hello! My name is Jimmy Nguyen. I am a student at UC Santa Cruz, majoring in Computer Science Game Design. As of right now, I am a senior, soon to be graduating next year. When I was in middle school, I attended an after-school program known as Boys and Girls Club to get further help with my assignments because my parents were unable to assist me. In this program, they offered activities once all the students had spent some time working on their homework. One of the activities they offered was an introduction to game design. The little course had us use Scratch, a game engine easy for kids to use. I enjoyed making games with Scratch, going beyond creating little games for my friends to play. This sparked my interest in game design, and from then on, I decided that I would study video game design. While Scratch was the gateway to video game design, I think it was also the time spent creating maps on Halo 3, Reach, and 4 Forge mode, and designing mini-game worlds in Minecraft that helped develop this passion for making games. Other than video game design, I also enjoy watching films and shows. My favorite genres would have to be Romance, Comedy, Horror, Slice of Life, and Mystery. I also enjoy watching anime, with the same genre taste, of course. A little expensive hobby of mine is cosplaying. I buy costumes and attend conventions that are hosted around the area. Cosplaying has opened me up to meeting people and expanding my social circle. It's really nice to find others who share similar interests while also learning something new about them. In the previous two years, I attended a convention called FanimeCon and hosted a fun little activity where I'd invite other con-goers to sit and have tea with me. It was so much fun, and I got to learn a lot from different people. I plan to keep doing this and am currently designing a little game to play at the convention.
